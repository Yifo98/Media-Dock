# Adopt the QIDU open-berth mark

Media Dock adopts the QIDU “泊” open-berth mark as its production identity: an open berth supports one compact media frame, with a restrained reflection indicating arrival and delivery. The mark keeps the existing Luminous Workshop palette and the Media Dock product name while adding the chapter name “泊”, the motto “泊其所获，交其所成。”, and the signature “A QIDU Utility”.

The production source is the text-free vector at `docs/design/assets/media-dock-qidu-berth.svg`. Platform assets may add a warm neutral app-icon tile, transparent optical padding, or size-specific simplification, but they may not change the berth silhouette, add platform or website marks, or bake brand text into small icons.

This decision supersedes ADR 0012 and ADR 0013 for the shipping icon. Their earlier Compact Media Berth images remain historical design records rather than active production masters. ADR 0011, ADR 0014, and the Luminous Workshop constraints in ADR 0004 remain in force.
