# Use Luminous Workshop as the brand language

Media Dock 3.0 uses “Luminous Workshop” as its visual language: calm light-first surfaces, warm neutral foundations, graphite typography, and restrained cyan-to-lilac media accents, with a complete derived dark mode. First launch uses the light theme; users may explicitly choose light, dark, or system-following behavior, and the app remembers that choice without silently enabling system following. Cyan identifies primary action and processing, lilac identifies selection and relation, amber identifies attention, and red is reserved for dangerous action. The product removes decorative background skins and avoids cyber-control-room styling, generic AI neon, card walls, excessive glass, and literal tool-logo imagery; accessible contrast, visible focus, reduced motion, depth, and restrained transitions communicate a source entering the dock, being processed, and arriving as a deliverable.
